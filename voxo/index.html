Why Choose Voxo?

Voxo HTML template is an apparently simple but highly functional tempalate designed for creating a flourisahing online business.

  • Easy to customize
  • Responsive Design
  • 900+ Google Fonts

Front-end Pages

Voxo has pre Built 6 clean and modern demos with attractive hover effects. All Pages are easy to customize with clean coding made in SASS. Every Page will work perfectly in every device like Desktop, Laptop, Tablets & Phones

Back-end Pages

Voxo has 25+ pages with an attractive Design. Admin pages are also available in dark mode and RTL mode. All Pages are easy to customize with clean coding made in SASS. Every Backend Pages will work perfectly in every devices

Invoice Template Pages

Invoicing templates are quick and simple. They are easy to interact with, fill out, customize, and save. Voxo comes with a clean and modern invoice with Export as PDF and Print option. Voxo has 3 Invoice templates.

Email Template Pages

Email Templates are used to gather and nurture leads, communicate with customers and clients. Voxo has 8+ Email templates which include Welcome, reset the password, product review, order success and many more..

Key Features

Voxo is an excellent theme develop with a concise approach. The code written is a clean and well structured. Create a Website with all powerful features

Dark Mode

Useful feature for Night Owls or people who work during the night. The dark background will put less strain on the eyes.

RTL Ready

Design your website LTR or RTL , Voxo supports multi-languages so its easy to design website on any layout such as arabic , urdu..

Auto Height Adjustable

Every store has different image size weather its tools or fashion we have covered all image size for all your store needs , it has auto resize image option in Voxo

Lazy Load

Make your page faster by using lazy loaded. Lazy Load is delays loading in long web pages. Images outside of viewport are not loaded until user scrolls to them.

Unlimited Color

Voxo comes with unlimited possibilities with color picker. Choose any color which suitable your website or store

Cookie Bar

Cookies are files used to track site activity. cookiebar makes it simple and clear to visitors that cookies are in use. Voxo has 3 types of cookie bar design.
